Daniella Gáti

I am a scholar and teacher of digital media, social media, algorithmic culture, electronic literature, and games--more broadly, the new worlds created by code, digital interfaces, AI, AR and VR. I examine these processes globally, in their culturally contingent diversity, and I bring queer theory to bear upon the new and transformed forms of marginalization in digital space. I currently work as Clinical Assistant Professor in the Writing Program at NYU Shanghai.
